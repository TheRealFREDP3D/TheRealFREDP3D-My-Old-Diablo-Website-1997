SPELL Description

Apocolypse		 destruction of all nearby enemies, not available in book form.

Bloodstar		 same spell as used by the succubus, will use both mana and life if you don't have a mana shield.

Bonespirit		 Takes away aproximatly one third of it's targets life.

Chain Lightning		 Targets any nearby enemy with lightning, very effective.

Charged Bolt		 dispurses slow moving packets of lightning

Elemental		 Sends a man on fire in search of your closest enemy

Fireball		 One of the most effective spells, it will cause extreme damage to the target.

Firebolt		 Not as powerfull as a fireball, but doesn't use nearly as much mana.

Firewall		 forms a wall of fire that will stop most beast, use it to trap and burn.

Flamewave		 A firewall in motion, causes some damage but normally not the best spell choice.

Flash		 takes a lot of mana to be effective and the only effects beast in close proximity to you.

Gaurdian		 produces a three headed dragon that shoots firebolts at the target nearest it.

Golem		 The Frankenstein of Jewish legend, will follow you wherever you go attacking anything in it's path.

Holybolt		 The spell of choice against the undead.

Inferno		 ignites the ground in front of you causing heavy damage to your foe.

Infravision		 all enemies become visable to you, only available in scroll or staff.

Lightning		 fires a lightning bolt directly at an enemy

Manashield		 all damage to your character is absorbed by mana instead of life.

Nova		 an extremely powerful ring of lightning, not available in book form.

Town Portal		 probably the most important spell of all, it's your access from town to any level.

Stonecurse		 having the Medussa effect on your targeted enemy, will turn all foes to stone except Diablo.
