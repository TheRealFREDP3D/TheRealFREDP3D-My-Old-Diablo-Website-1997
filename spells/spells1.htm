  Regardless of your character type, Magic will play a predominant role in your war against the army of darkness. Whether you use simpler forms of magic to heal yourself (or other party members) after mega battles, or whether you unleash fiery bolts of damnation from your fingertips, it will bode well for you to learn all the dark secrets that originated from the ancient mage-clans of the Eastern World.

Availble in scrolls, staves, and learned spells, there are indeed many forms of Magic to be found throughout the lands you will journey. You are able to "learn" spells by finding and reading spell books throughout the game. Reading additional spell books of a particular type will increase the spell level up to a maximum of level 15. The higher the spell level, the more mackdaddy the spell. Magical shrines may also be used to increase spell level as well.

Learn as much as you can about Magic, fine warrior -for it will take more than a clenched fist and a blade of steel to take down the evil Diablo and return peace to the troubled village of Tristam.

Apocalypse

Converts all enemies within certain range into individual little nuclear holocausts. Groovy! Not available as a learned spell - only available by using scrolls or staves.

N/A

Blood Star

Looks just like the spell those damn witches use in Hell. Argh! Does damage equal to 1/3 of caster's total Magic points, so as your Magic attribute rises, so does the damage level of this spell.

Spell uses less mana. Magic stars travel faster.

Bone Spirit

Takes 1/3 total hit points from target each time it hits. Excellent spell to use on any enemy with mega hit points.

Spell uses less mana.

Chain Lightning

 Multiple bolts of lightning fly from caster's fingertips inflicting massive amounts of damage to anyone (friend or foe) that happens to get in the way. Great spell to quickly disintegrate everything in a room. Watch out for characters immune to lightning attacks though!

Spell uses less mana. More streams of lightning and more damage as well.

Charged Bolt

Multiple charges of lightning are emitted and bounce around until they hit an enemy or run out of steam. You'll use this in the beginning of the game but it will quickly grow obsolete when you learn Chain Lightning.

Spell uses less mana. More charges are unleashed with longer duration.

Elemental

Caster conjures up a little running man of flames that auto-targets enemy. We've taken the liberty of renaming this spell the "Flaming Deion!" Just use it - you'll see what we mean...

Spell uses less mana. More damage is inflicted.

Fireball

The big kahuna mackdaddy. You wanna toast something? Use this. Often a single ball of fire can take out multiple opponents. This is the perfect weapon against the witches in Hell. Just make sure your buddies are out of the way!

Spell uses less mana. More damage is inflicted.

Firebolt

The little brother of Fireball. Useful, but not near as strong. Only inflicts damage on a single target.

Spell uses less mana. Bolts are faster and do more damage.

Firewall

Stationary wall of fire that inflicts mega damage on large groups of enemies trapped in a room. Cast multiple times in a row for full effect.

Spell uses less mana and has longer duration.

Flame Wave

Wave of flame that moves in the direction it was cast damaging all that dare enter its path. Great spell in hallways or for casting into a new room. Even if you don't kill all the monsters, you hurt them and make them easier to take out by hand.

Spell uses less mana and has increased area of effect.

Flash

Energy orb explodes damaging all enemies immediately surrounding the caster. Pro is that the spell gets powerful later in the game. Con is that is does not affect the undead.

Spell uses less mana and more damage is inflicted.

Golem

One of our personal faves! Cast this spell and out of the ground grows a stone dude to help you fight. If you let him, he can darn near take out entire dungeons by himself. Golem is downright groovy! Chicks dig him!

 Spell uses less mana. Golem lasts longer.

Guardian

Three-headed dragon emerges from the ground and shoots fireballs at all enemies within range. Great for doorways. Try casting multiple times, stand among the dragons, and cast Stone Curse as the enemies draw near. Awesome.

Spell uses less mana while increasing in damage and duration.

Healing

Use to restore personal hit points. Mmm, mmm, good.

Spell uses less mana and provides greater healing effect.

Heal Other

Use to restore hit points to other members of your party. This spell is (obviously) only available in multiplayer mode.

Spell uses less mana and provides greater healing effect.

Holy Bolt

Fires magical bolts that only harm the undead. Nice spell when playing multiplayer because you won't singe your homies...

Spell uses less mana while increasing in speed and damage.

Identify

Reveals all characteristics of any unknown magical item. Only available in scroll form.

N/A

Inferno

Cool special effects - bogus spell. Don't use it.

Spell uses less mana while increasing range, damage, and duration.

Infravision

Let's you see monsters that would normally be hidden by darkness. They glow red when you are under the influence of this spell. Great spell for dark dungeons but cannot be "learned" by reading a book.

N/A

Lightning

Caster conjures single bolt of lightning from sky and directs it smack-dab onto the forehead of an unsuspecting enemy. Ouch!

Spell uses less mana and increases in damage.

Mana Shield

Great for a Sorcerer, useless for everyone else. Casting this spell allows personal damage to decrease your mana pool rather than your hit points. Since high-level Sorcerers can have over 800 mana points, this spell makes them virtually invincible in later levels of the game.

Spell uses less mana.

Nova

Sphere of lightning explodes and travels until it hits a wall damaging everything along the way. Cannot be "learned" by reading a book.

N/A

Phasing

Teleport spell with random effects. Unpredictable by nature but can be used to escape when surrounded by an angry mob.

Spell uses less mana.

Resurrect

Brings dead party member back to life. Never leave home without it. Cannot be "learned" by reading a book.

N/A
Stone Curse

Excellent, excellent spell. Turns your enemy into stone thus freezing him for a few seconds so you can freely hack away at him. Works on all enemies except Diablo. Use it and use it often.

Spell uses less mana and has increased duration.

Telekenesis

Allows the caster to move objects and monsters from a distance. Can be used to safely disarm traps from a distance. Use the force Luke...

Spell uses less mana and grows in strength.

Teleport

Much cooler teleport spell than Phasing. Use to escape angry hordes of monsters, cross rivers of lava, etc. Very accurate spell. Most excellent!

Spell uses less mana.

Town Portal

Opens mystic portal that allows travel from dungeons back to town. Great for quick escapes or mana runs.

 Spell uses less mana.
